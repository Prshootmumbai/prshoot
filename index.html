The Power of Storytelling
Every moment of our lives is a new story unfolding in it’s own unique way.

We all experience it all the time, but only a few can express and showcase it in a way that can communicate and inspire others. A well told story can not only make a message reach to millions but can even change the course of history. When Mahatma Gandhi called for “Do or Die” or Churchill gave a sign of “V for Victory”, millions of people stood up against a great oppressive power in an instance, inspired and motivated. That’s the power of storytelling. PR Shoot, Mumbai, is a production company which takes the medium of filmmaking in order to help you tell your story, spread your message to your target audience and to reach your goals whether it’s for your business growth, for spreading awareness on a topic or for the sake of storytelling itself. We believe in making very personalized videos so that it’s exactly about you and your needs. Whether it’s social media content, internal communications or a full brand campaign, our films bring your stories to life. We love a challenge and offer various creative filmmaking methods to bring out the best out of a story

Pre/Post Production
01

Pre Production

Every creation starts with an idea, and once we have it, we develop it into a perfect plan. Our creative team work their magic to convert your idea into reality. To convert the inspiration into a perfect project, we plan everything meticulously from storyboards, scripts and designs to booking talents, studios, logistics, shooting kits and every small details. The tone of the project is also decided in this stage including music and motion graphics style. We love it when a plan comes together.
02

Production

Whether we are shooting in a studio or in a real location, flying drones or capturing a time-lapse, we are always excited when the camera rolls. If you’re starring in the film, our camera crew and production team will guide you throughout the process. If not, you are also welcome to join us as we capture that perfect shot.
03

Post Production

Once the shoot is done, your film starts taking shape as we edit and polish it. Colour grading, titles and motion graphics, sound effects, voiceovers and music: we make sure it all looks and sounds exactly as you wanted . Because we want you to be as happy with the final product as we are, we offer multiple amends on each project.

About Us

We’re a specialist film production company, based in Mumbai and working across India and abroad as well. Our team includes expert producers, editors, camera operators, motion designers and drone pilots – all the skills needed to turn your brief into high-quality creative work. As a specialist production company, our mission is to connect people through film. And we’ll work with you to find the best way to do that. Maybe you are planning a product launch or social media campaign, or need to communicate a vital message to staff or customers. You know what you want to achieve, but what’s the next step? Whatever stage your brief is at, we’ll use our expertise in all areas of production to offer creative and practical solutions. If a beautifully shot and edited video is the answer, we are onto it. If you need a photo campaign , an eye-catching time-lapse sequence, dramatic drone footage – or a mix of all of those – we’ll provide them too. We can arrange locations and actors, voiceovers and even source the perfect props. We pride ourselves on offering clear and straightforward pricing with no hidden costs. And because our team comes with its own kit, your budget goes on quality, not rentals.
